Featured Projects

Patent Project

I designed and developed a real-time hardware system titled "Animals Train Collision Prevention" to mitigate train-animal collisions. This project integrates sensors and embedded systems to detect animals on railway tracks and alert train operators, demonstrating a practical and impactful solution to a critical issue.

Smart Irrigation System

I developed and implemented a real-time hardware solution titled "Smart Irrigation System", designed to optimize water usage in agriculture. The system integrates soil moisture sensors and microcontrollers to monitor environmental conditions and control water pumps automatically. This project enhances irrigation efficiency, reduces water wastage, and supports sustainable agricultural practices.

Automatic Street Light Control System

I designed and built an "Automatic Street Light Control System", which uses LDR sensors and microcontrollers to automatically adjust street lighting based on ambient light levels. This project significantly reduces energy consumption, ensures cost-effectiveness, and provides a reliable, efficient street lighting solution for urban and rural areas.

Valuto: Account Management System

I developed a CLI system for accounting management and financial statement maintenance. The purpose of the system is to maintain a record of the funds received, used, disseminated and donated by an organization. Details of transactions and maintenance of a budget and tallying of current balance/inventory in cash/kind is incorporated in this system.
